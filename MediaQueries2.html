About

At Entrepreneurship cell NITH, we believe in bridging the gap between who we are and who we want to be. Our prime objective is to help the students who are eager to become the entrepreneurs of future in developing successful business ideas and hence transmuting them into their physical form.With just the right amount of aspiration,perseverance and execution, we hope to transform the individuals with creative minds from a set of "job seekers" to "job creators"

Vision

E-CELL NITH has the vision to create an entrepreneurial ecosystem for budding entrepreneurs and promote entrepreneurial growth in NITH. We aim to create a place where students can access entrepreneurial resources, network with community entrepreneurs, and share ideas. We are here to nurture, refine, incubate as well as facilitate nascent business ventures from NITH.

Mission

Entrepreneurship and Business Development in Himachal region in our alma-mater, National Institute of Technology Hamirpur (H.P.). We aim to reshape our annual financial structure to make it ‘THE’ college Start-up Accelerator platform for anyone involved in or with Entrepreneurship and Business Development in North India.
